Mission Statement

To establish the Jamaica Rising Parade as an annual, internationally recognized cultural celebration that honors Jamaica's rich history, amplifies its artistic and civic contributions, unites the diaspora, fosters youth engagement, and educates the broader public through vibrant expressions of music, dance, food, fashion, and national pride.

Parade
Route

Brooklyn Route Proposal

Brooklyn remains a vibrant cultural epicenter for Jamaican Americans, especially in Flatbush, Crown Heights, and East Flatbush. The route will honor this historical footprint.

Start: Church Avenue & Flatbush Avenue.
Proceed North: Along Flatbush, Brooklyn's central artery.
End: Parkside Avenue

Note: The Parade route is subject to change.

Founder

Dr. Bill Tinglin

Dr. Bill Tinglin is a Jamaican-American educator, humanitarian and author whose deep commitment to cultural pride and community service has shaped his work. His Jamaican upbringing, combined with his years of international advocacy for education, tolerance and diaspora engagement, laid the foundation for his role as Chairperson of the Jamaica Rising Day Parade.
